About

NCCF is a Christ-centered, Bible-based, Spirit-filled, disciple-making, mission-driven and a caring church.

We are committed to honor God and expand His Kingdom on earth through personal witnessing, cell group experience, evangelism activities, prayer and our church outreaches.

We welcome anyone who is seeking the Lord Jesus. NCCF is willing to share the love and life we gained from serving Him alone.

Worship Service: Sundays 9-11AM

Prayer Meetings: Mon 7-8PM; Tues 6-7PM; Thur 7-8PM

Cell Leaders Weekly Meeting: Thurs 8-9PM

Music Avenue

Music Avenue is a passionate, dynamic, music-oriented and Godly youth movement under New Creation Christian Fellowship (NCCF) that uplifts the talents of youth especially in music. It offers basic and advance lessons and workshops to maximize their ability to its full potential.

Lastly, it imparts biblical moral values that will impact their lives, community and campus.
