WHERE DO YOU START?

We welcome anyone who is seeking the Lord Jesus. Any new situation can be confusing when you are not sure where to go or what to do. There are plenty of ways to get involved, NCCF is willing to share the love and life we gained from serving Him alone.

NCCF is a Christ-centered, Bible-based, Spirit-filled, disciple-making, mission-driven and a caring church. We are committed to honor God and expand His Kingdom on earth through personal witnessing, cell group experience, evangelism activities, prayer and our church outreaches.
